“Self-love is the best remedy.”

“Self-love is the best remedy.”

This article is based on my life experiences, and as we enter the year 2024, it is important to prioritize ourselves as much as possible. While family and friends can bring us happiness, it is important to figure out our happiness. What happens if we are left with no real friends or they betray us? Unfortunately, in this modern world, not only lovers but friends can abandon us as well. This article can be an eye-opener to take action towards our happiness.Here are some signs that our close friends are not there for us anymore, and we should focus more on ourselves: 1. They don’t communicate with us anymore: (Text/ Call/ E-mail/ In-person) 2. They avoid meeting us: (They don’t make plans/ Cancel plans/ Don’t put effort/ Exclude us from plans) 3. They hide things from us: (Lying/ Making excuses/ Not telling us things intentionally) 4. They pretend to befriend us but they are not genuine: Saying sorry again and again to hide or cover their mistakes. Remember — when someone says sorry again and again, the value of sorry diminishes. It doesn’t mean that they should start using synonyms for sorry, haha. 5. Texting for work purposes only: They only text or call us when they need our help. 6. Texting on occasions and festivals only: It seems meaningless if they only remember us on special occasions. 7. Gaslighting: Giving an explanation that we are overthinking and they did nothing wrong rather than accepting their mistake.
